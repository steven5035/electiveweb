Why is it important?
It helps people to brain storm what they want to create that might solve their problems

3 things to consider :Snytactic ,Pragmatic ,Semantic
Snytactic is about materail and diamention of a material
Pragmatic is about how the item works
Semantic is about how it heps people



Examples

This is a paper detector ,it is made of wood box with ultrasonic sensor and led (snytactic).
It detect if there is paper ,if paper is less than half of the box ,the led will light up (pragmatic)
This device is to make sure paper are always avaliable to people who needed it (semantic)
